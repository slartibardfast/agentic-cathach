- Status: review, addressed

- Serves: the whole cast, with [Bríd](../../cast/brid-archivist.md) primary

The six personas reviewed the milestone from the humanist standpoint: the project as
its human readers meet it, on the page and across the horizon of
[call/0015](../../call/0015-longevity-and-positioning.md). This is a review record,
not a decision. The decisions it cites govern; where this record and a `call/` entry
disagree, the decision wins.

## What the cast affirmed

The format treats its future readers honestly. The longevity claim is scoped to what
the record supports, the hand digest states what it cannot prove, zero is a positive
mark because a blank cannot be told from damage, and the teaching assumes nothing
above counting. The cast shaped the decisions rather than decorated them: Nuala's
figure-carries-the-load standard and Cormac's guard-arithmetic point are visible in
[call/0009](../../call/0009-payload-symbols-and-guards.md) and
[call/0013](../../call/0013-preamble-pedagogy-and-floor.md), and the operator's hand
approved the alphabet so no algorithm chose the marks a human will trace.
